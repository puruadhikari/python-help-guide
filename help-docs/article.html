Welcome and Annual Benefits

Earn a significant bonus of Membership Rewards Points upon meeting initial spending requirements. Cardmembers can also receive an annual travel credit and a global dining credit, which can be used at a wide range of participating restaurants.

Membership Rewards Program

The card offers a robust rewards program, allowing you to earn points on every purchase. These points can be redeemed for flights, hotel stays, gift cards, and more. You can earn an accelerated rate on specific categories like international spending or purchases with partner brands.

Travel and Lounge Access

Enjoy unlimited, complimentary access to over 1,400 airport lounges worldwide through the American Express Global Lounge Collection. This includes The Centurion Lounge, Priority Pass lounges, Escape Lounges, and more. The card also provides benefits with rental car companies and complimentary travel insurance.
